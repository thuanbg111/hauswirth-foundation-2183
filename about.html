AboutHAUSWIRTH FOUNDATION

HAUSWIRTH FOUNDATION is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 883732183) formed to bring practical support to people in transition. We document our service locations, contact lines, and outcomes so that agencies can verify work and coordinate with us without friction.
